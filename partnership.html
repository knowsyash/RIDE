Partnership Rules and Norms

Our Business Model

At COLLAGE-KART, we offer partnership opportunities for individuals or organizations interested in promoting our platform and expanding our reach to college campuses.

Commission Rates

We offer competitive commission rates based on the sales generated through your referrals. The commission rate may vary depending on the products and services sold.

Partnership Benefits

  • Access to a wide range of products and services to promote.
  • Flexible commission structures tailored to your preferences.
  • Dedicated support team to assist you throughout the partnership.
  • Regular updates on promotions, discounts, and new products.
  • Opportunity to earn additional bonuses based on performance.

Partner Responsibilities

  • Promote COLLAGE-KART platform and products through various channels.
  • Adhere to ethical marketing practices and represent our brand with integrity.
  • Provide timely feedback and suggestions for improving our partnership program.
  • Abide by the terms and conditions outlined in the partnership agreement.
